Welcome!

Siripura Central College, a major school located in the Dibulagala Educational Zone of the historic Polonnaruwa District of the North Central Province, is a great place for children in the area to succeed in their educational activities. The educational activities of the college are implemented under two sections: primary and secondary. A vocational education section, which was started under the 13-year continuous education project, is also implemented in our school. Our school is among the first schools in the region to start this section.
